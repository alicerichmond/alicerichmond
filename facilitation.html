Facilitation

Creative workshopping

Is about creating a safe and inviting space for everyone to feel empowered to have a voice. This space can be used to create, nurture or develop ideas or have those difficult conversations

Make it fun

Without the proper attention a workshop can be arduous or have low participant contribution. I utilise gamification, learning design and collaborative workshopping techniques for both remote and in person sessions. This allows a much more equal distribution of power, greater conversation and more productive workshops.

I design and lead immersive inception sessions, UX workshops, stakeholder workshops, service blueprinting sessions and many more.

Show don't tell

I use "show don't tell", as the guiding ethos, emobodying the behaviour, ideas, processes and approaches that I see generating positivity and collaboration.
